“this was the essential love of Nature in me, this the root of all that I have usefully become, and the light of all that I have rightly learned.”

‘Beyond’, represents my take on what the world will look like if quarantine due to the COVID 19 Pandemic doesn’t end. The series features black & white imagery that depicts famous urban landmarks, being overrun by rich vegetation.

In this project, I would like to introduce you to the world I’ve imagined, in which mythical places would become almost unrecognizable, while flourishing nature takes over its rights. Monuments like Saint Peter’s Basilica, the Eiffel Tower, San Marco square, or the Statue of Liberty are represented in this series from a new wild perspective, showcasing the effects of a global quarantine which lasts longer than expected.
