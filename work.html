Beyond
2020

 “What the world will look like if
quarantine doesn’t end”

‘Beyond’, is one of my collections of images. It represents my take on what the world would look like if quarantine due to the COVID-19 Pandemic wouldn’t have ended. The series features black & white imagery that depicts famous urban landmarks, being overrun by rich vegetation.
